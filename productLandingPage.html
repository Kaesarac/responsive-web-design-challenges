About Skepta: If I had to resume myself up in one word, i'd rather use an Emoji.


“When I started out I was metaphorically shouting, not just with my music, but through my clothing, because I just wanted to be seen, heard and respected.”
Skepta explains, pointing out his previous love of big Moschino prints
“That was my favourite swag” – Ed Hardy and Evisu. “Patterns and logos were my thing. I was trying to scream out to the world for people to see and listen to me.”
Then he started to be heard, he says, and he began to tone it down.

For Skepta, his successes have allowed him to quell his ambition to be heard.
“I'm happy and at peace with who I am and what I am,” he told GQ.
But the 38-year-old MC is also at peace with others as well:
“I don't feel the need to tell somebody that they shouldn't act a certain way towards me.”
